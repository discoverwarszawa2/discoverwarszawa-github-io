This project was initiated when I first moved to Warsaw in May 2015. During my time there I visited many great places, and wanted to document these to keep a memory of my movements during this time.

It was also an opportunity for me to implement the knowledge I had gained with the Jekyll 'static site generator'.

When I arrived back in the UK I began researching JavaScript frameworks, with a large focus put on AngularJS. With this new knowledge I decided to redesign this website to include the AngularJS framework.
